Strategy Development

Creating effective growth and business development strategies

When Do You Need Strategy Development

You need to understand - is it better to develop in the current market or seek new niches and segments? Grow in your region or expand to other cities and countries?

You want to determine which business directions to close and which to strengthen, how better to grow - organically or through M&A?

You want to determine the expansion format: develop a network, work through distributors, develop franchising, open branches, acquire competitors or create JVs with strategic partners?

Our Approach

Strategic Goals

Formulate and align stakeholders' strategic goals. Define development priorities and key performance indicators for a 3-5 year horizon.

Market Analysis

Analyze the value chain and structure of the market where the company operates. Identify key segments, their capacity, dynamics and key trends. Assess the company's current competitive position.

Risk Assessment

Understand fundamental strategic risks for the key market. Determine how sustainable and long-term its growth is.

Strategic Directions

Propose strategic directions that will achieve the company's strategic goals with minimal financial and time costs.

Strategic Plan

Prepare and quantify the company's strategic plan, align it with all stakeholders.

Development Model

Provide recommendations on the company's development model within new strategic directions

What the Client Gets

Complete Understanding of Strategic Alternatives and Goals

Clear vision of possible development directions and priority company goals

Clear Action Plan for Strategy Implementation

Detailed roadmap with concrete steps to achieve set goals

Financial Model with Key KPIs

Company strategy reflected in numbers and specific performance indicators
